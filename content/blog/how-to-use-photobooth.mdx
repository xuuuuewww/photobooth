# How to Use Online Photobooth

Creating a stylish photo strip takes only a few steps.

## 1. Capture your photos

Open the camera flow and take a full set of shots. Keep your face centered and use even lighting for the cleanest output.

## 2. Customize your strip

Choose a template, background color, stickers, and filters to match your style. Add footer text if you want a timestamp or message.

## 3. Export and share

Download your final image or use the share actions to generate a public link. You can then post it on social media or send it to friends.

## Pro tips

- Use soft natural light for better skin tones.
- Keep the camera at eye level for flattering framing.
- Try multiple templates before exporting to find the best vibe.
